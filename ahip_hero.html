NOTES
For AHIP's social media hero images I decided to use warm, diverse imagery, adding a duotone overlay to suggest approchability and youthfulness (I also think the duotone distracts from the stock-y nature of the photos, and adds a subtle Rad flavor). Furthering those themes I chose to accent our trusty blue with the happy and eye-catching orange in AHIP's palette.

Before I arrived at AHIP, the bright colors in their palette were either being used extremely sparingly in otherwise grey and blue reports, or in shocking combinations and patterns over in the marketing department. In all of my designs at AHIP I was able to synthesize these bright colors with an accessible, diplomatic DC tone that spoke to both our public and political audiences.

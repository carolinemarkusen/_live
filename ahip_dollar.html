NOTES
A lot of what I did at AHIP was update existing designs with new data. This infographic is a great example of updating an old design to evoke bright, streamlined energy consistent with AHIP's new forward-thinking brand. It had the highest engagement of any post across AHIP's social platforms for three months running, and was repurposed by numerous media outlets.

I created a plethora of marketing materials from this infographic, including versions formatted for social media, e-mail graphics, sidebar advertisements, newsletters and PowerPoint. I decided to leave them out of this page as they are extremely repetitive; we'll save our eyes from an overload of AHIP's astonishingly rainbow color palette.

The Brenton Arboretum's mission is clear and simple: to bring joy to all through the beauty and knowledge of the natural world of trees. The Arboretum asked me to design a logo for their twentieth-anniversary bacchanal. It was used for all marketing materials including invitations, t-shirts, stickers, posters and flyers.
